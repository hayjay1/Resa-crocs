Who We Are

Our Story

Chapter One

The Idea.

In 2021, our founder walked into a meeting in a pair of classic Crocs and was told — politely but firmly — to go home and change. She didn't. Resa was born from that refusal.

The idea was simple: what if the world's most comfortable shoe was also the most beautiful? What if ease and elegance weren't opposites, but collaborators?

Chapter Two

The Craft.

Every Resa begins as raw EVA foam, shaped to our proprietary silhouette and finished by hand in our Lagos Island atelier. The process takes four times longer than mass production.

That's not inefficiency. That's intention.

The most radical thing a Lagos woman can do is be completely, unapologetically comfortable.
